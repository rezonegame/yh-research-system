# Self-Play Iteration 1

## Challenger Tasks

1. A historic commercial block asks for a "fun route" but has no official floorplan, no merchant list, and no heritage boundary confirmation.
2. A cultural-tourism route wants to use resident stories but only has public media summaries and no consented interviews.
3. A district wants an app/AR scavenger hunt with only one part-time operator and low merchant willingness.

## Judge Diagnosis

The v0.1 skill handled project structure well but did not force a missing-material ledger early enough. It could produce attractive proposals before clearly labeling official facts, assumptions, and must-request materials.

## Proposed Repair

Add evidence gates, material request template, and release labels that stop exploratory concepts from being mistaken for publish-ready outputs.
