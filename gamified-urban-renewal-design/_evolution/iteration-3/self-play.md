# Self-Play Iteration 3

## Challenger Tasks

1. A 15-minute neighbourhood project wants a serious game, but participants include elders and busy merchants with little time.
2. A low-budget district has GIS data but no ability to maintain a digital twin after the pilot.
3. A commercial renewal project wants return visits, but the proposed tasks burden shop staff and become discount-driven.

## Judge Diagnosis

The skill needed a stronger "smallest adequate medium" principle and a lightweight operations module. It should recommend passports, stamps, QR pages, and low-burden merchant levels before custom apps or sensors.

## Proposed Repair

Add lightweight local operations reference, operating-plan template, merchant burden levels, return-loop design, and operational lightness as a quality score.
