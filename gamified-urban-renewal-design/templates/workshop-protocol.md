## 4. Facilitation Rules

- Make constraints explicit before gameplay.
- Keep rules learnable.
- Track quiet voices and dominant voices.
- Ask for reasons behind choices.
- Record conflict without forcing false consensus.
- Explain what will happen after the workshop.

## 6. Post-Workshop Process

1. Clean and categorize raw outputs.
2. Translate game evidence into design options.
3. Conduct expert feasibility review.
4. Return options for validation.
5. Revise and integrate into master proposal.
