## 8. Debrief Questions

1. What did your group prioritize and why?
2. What did you remove or reject and why?
3. Where did conflict appear?
4. Whose needs were easiest to satisfy?
5. Whose needs were hardest to satisfy?
6. What would make this proposal feasible in reality?

## 9. Failure Checks

- Is the game too easy to reach consensus?
- Are rules hiding real constraints?
- Are some participants likely to dominate?
- Are points replacing meaningful discussion?
- Can outputs be translated into design decisions?
