# Sukhomlinsky Publish-Ready Bibliography

## Primary and Near-Primary Works

| Source | Use in skill |
|---|---|
| `My Heart I Give to Children` / `To Children I Give My Heart` | Child-centered practice, school of joy, nature, reading, beauty, empathy, health, literacy support. |
| `Pavlysh Secondary School` / `Our School in Pavlysh` | Whole-school system: health, moral, intellectual, work, aesthetic education and their interconnections. |
| `100 Pieces of Advice for Teachers` | Teacher vocation, teacher craft, the teacher's word and moral responsibility. |
| `Birth of a Citizen` | Adolescent moral/civic formation; included as future bibliography expansion. |
| `Letters to My Son` | Personal-moral formation; included as future bibliography expansion. |

## Bibliographic Gaps

- Direct Russian/Ukrainian editions are not fully locally digested.
- `Birth of a Citizen` and `Letters to My Son` need deeper local notes for a stronger adolescence/citizenship model.
- Some Chinese reception sources are summaries and should not override primary writings.
