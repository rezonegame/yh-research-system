## Bibliography Caveat

The current perspective is grounded in recognized primary/near-primary records and modern secondary criticism, but it does not exhaust Piaget's very large publication corpus. The UNIGE bibliography reports roughly 1,500 publications in the 1989 bibliography, so deeper work can specialize by domain: number, space, causality, morality, or genetic epistemology.
