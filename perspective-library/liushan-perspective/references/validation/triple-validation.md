# Triple Validation

## Model 1: 把神像搬到饭桌旁

- Cross-domain recurrence: Appears in divine dialogues, underworld dialogues, mock auctions, and social satires.
- Predictive power: For a new authority claim, the model predicts Lucianic analysis will move it into ordinary settings and strip status.
- Exclusivity: More specific than generic skepticism because the mechanism is theatrical deflation through setting change.
- Status: Promoted.

## Model 2: 荒诞是测谎仪

- Cross-domain recurrence: *True History* uses explicit lying; related fantastic voyage and impossible detail recur in moon/sky/underworld episodes.
- Predictive power: For a suspicious narrative, the model predicts attention to how detail manufactures credibility.
- Exclusivity: Distinctively Lucianic because it uses admitted fiction to expose disguised fiction.
- Status: Promoted.

## Model 3: 把哲学拿去拍卖

- Cross-domain recurrence: *Philosophies for Sale*, *The Fisherman*, *Hermotimus*, and anti-philosopher passages across the corpus.
- Predictive power: For a doctrine, the model asks what it costs, who buys it, and how it behaves outside its own vocabulary.
- Exclusivity: More operational than generic anti-philosophy; it tests doctrines as lived and sold packages.
- Status: Promoted.

## Model 4: 史家必须不怕主人

- Cross-domain recurrence: Explicit in *How to Write History*; reinforced by *True History*'s parody of false reporting and repeated attacks on rhetorical imposture.
- Predictive power: For reports, histories, and institutional narratives, the model detects panegyric and evidence gaps.
- Exclusivity: Distinguishes Lucian from pure comic satirists by adding truth obligations in narration.
- Status: Promoted.

## Model 5: 骗子靠观众共同完成

- Cross-domain recurrence: *Alexander*, *Peregrinus*, ghost/miracle tales, and philosophy marketplace scenes all involve audience desire.
- Predictive power: For scams or hype, the model looks beyond the impostor to believers, incentives, and social appetite.
- Exclusivity: Strongly Lucianic because fraud is staged as social theatre, not only false proposition.
- Status: Promoted.

## Model 6: 边缘人的双语透镜

- Cross-domain recurrence: Biographical origin at Samosata plus deep Greek literary performance and repeated internal critique of Greek cultural authorities.
- Predictive power: For insider jargon, the model predicts a dual reading: mastery of terms plus outsider deflation.
- Exclusivity: Distinguishes Lucian from center-born Greek moralists and from external polemicists.
- Status: Promoted with medium confidence due to biographical uncertainty.

## Rejected / Limited Claims

- "Lucian was a modern atheist": rejected. Evidence supports anti-imposture and mythological satire, not direct identity with modern atheism.
- "Lucian invented science fiction": limited. *True History* is an important ancestor of fantastic/science-fictional travel, but the modern genre label is retrospective.
- "Lucian hated all philosophy": rejected. Works attack false philosophers and dogmatic posturing; the critique uses philosophical intelligence.
