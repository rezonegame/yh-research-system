# Socrates Publish-Ready Bibliography

This bibliography supports publish-ready review for `socrates-perspective`. It separates primary, near-primary, external-critical, and modern secondary sources.

## Primary and Near-Primary Texts

| Source | Use in skill |
|---|---|
| Plato, `Apology` | Public replay anchor for Socratic ignorance, examination of wisdom, care of the soul, and refusal of courtroom manipulation. |
| Plato, `Crito` | Public replay anchor for principle consistency and refusal to answer injustice with injustice. |
| Plato, `Meno` | Definition-first inquiry, aporia, guided discovery, and teachability of virtue. |
| Plato, `Euthyphro` | Definition before judgment, piety inquiry, counterexample pressure. |
| Plato, `Laches`, `Charmides`, `Protagoras`, `Gorgias`, `Hippias Minor`, `Ion`, `Republic` Book I | Wider elenctic-dialogue evidence for definition-seeking and interlocutor-owned premises. |
| Xenophon, `Memorabilia` | Practical moral Socrates: self-command, usefulness to companions, applied questioning. |
| Xenophon, `Apology` and `Symposium` | Alternative Socratic portrait; helps avoid Plato-only overfitting. |
| Aristophanes, `Clouds` | Hostile public caricature; useful for anti-patterns around sophistry and clever evasion. |
| Aristotle, selected reports on Socrates | Later ancient testimony on definitions and induction; lower-confidence than Plato/Xenophon but useful in the Socratic problem. |

## Modern Secondary Sources to Consult for Further Hardening

These are not all fully digested into the current skill, but they define the next bibliography expansion path.

| Author / Work | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Gregory Vlastos, `Socrates: Ironist and Moral Philosopher` | Classic analytic reconstruction of Socratic philosophy and irony. |
| Gregory Vlastos, "The Socratic Elenchus" | Foundational modern account of elenchus as method. |
| Hugh H. Benson, `Essays on the Philosophy of Socrates` | Important collection on Socratic method and doctrine. |
| Thomas C. Brickhouse and Nicholas D. Smith, `Plato's Socrates` | Major reconstruction of Socrates in Plato; useful for doctrine/method boundaries. |
| Thomas C. Brickhouse and Nicholas D. Smith, `Socrates on Trial` | Trial interpretation and historical/legal context. |
| Gail Fine, ed., `Plato 1: Metaphysics and Epistemology` | Includes influential treatments relevant to Socratic/Platonic distinction. |
| Gerasimos Santas, `Socrates` | Argument reconstruction of Socratic dialogues. |
| William Prior, ed., `Socrates: Critical Assessments` | Broad scholarly coverage: Socratic problem, ignorance, trial, method, virtue. |
| Debra Nails, `The People of Plato` | Prosopography for interlocutors and historical context. |
| Paul Woodruff, work on Plato's shorter ethical works | Useful for elenchus rules and dialogue interpretation. |
