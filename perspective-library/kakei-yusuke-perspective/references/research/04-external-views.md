# 04 External Views - Yusuke Kakei / 筧裕介

Research date: 2026-05-09

## External descriptions

- anow describes issue+design as an inventor group that works on social issues through design, emphasizing encounters and ecological approaches. Confidence: high. Source: https://www.anow.jp/business/14/
- Eiji Press biography frames him as a practitioner/researcher of social design for social problems and notes awards for `できますゼッケン` and `親子健康手帳`. Confidence: high. Source: https://eijipress.co.jp/products/2128
- Keio Sensing & Design Lab lists him as special appointed professor focused on design for medical, welfare, education, environmental and regional revitalization issues. Confidence: high. Source: https://www.kohtake.sdm.keio.ac.jp/member/
- Business Chronicle frames issue+design's major current themes as dementia and decarbonization. Confidence: high. Source: https://business-chronicle.com/person/issueplusdesign.php

## Common praise pattern

Observers praise Kakei not mainly for visual style, but for translating hard, negative, technical themes into forms that ordinary citizens can enter: books, games, handbooks, programs, maps and workshops.

## Possible criticism / limitation inferred from sources

- Many projects create awareness and participation, but deep structural change can remain slow. Kakei himself notes that even after high sales and NHK broadcasts, dementia prejudice has not disappeared. Confidence: high. Source: https://i-life.net/column/dementia_world/
- The method relies on strong encounters with credible domain partners; without them, the designer can lack enough legitimacy or ground truth. Confidence: high, partly inferred from his own comments. Sources: https://www.anow.jp/business/14/ and https://i-life.net/column/dementia_world/
