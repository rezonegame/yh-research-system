# 05 Decisions - 崔愷

Research date: 2026-05-09

## Key career decisions and project logic

- Worked within major national design institutions rather than as a purely private signature architect. This shaped a pragmatic, engineering-aware and public-responsibility oriented worldview. Confidence: high.
- Developed "本土设计" as a long-term theoretical/practice position. Decision logic: Chinese contemporary architecture should build confidence through transformation of local conditions rather than copying Western modernism or ancient motifs. Confidence: high.
- Took on major public cultural and civic buildings. Decision logic: public architecture must balance image, civic function, construction feasibility and urban context. Confidence: high.
- Engaged with green building and low-carbon discourse. Decision logic: sustainability must be part of design method and lifecycle performance, not a decorative technical add-on. Confidence: high.
- Participated in urban renewal discussions/projects including historic urban districts. Decision logic: renew by repair, continuity and careful insertion where possible, instead of erasing urban memory. Confidence: medium-high.
- 2025 Osaka Expo China Pavilion design public reporting connects him to bamboo-slip imagery / traditional textual memory and contemporary low-carbon display. Decision logic: national cultural symbols should be transformed into spatial and material systems, not copied literally. Confidence: medium-high.

## Decision pattern

Read site and social task -> identify local cultural/climatic/material logic -> coordinate function, structure, technology and cost -> restrain image-making -> test long-term operation and public value.

## Tensions

- National/public representation vs everyday usability.
- Cultural confidence vs superficial symbolism.
- Green ambition vs actual construction/operation performance.
- Urban renewal visibility vs slow, careful repair.
