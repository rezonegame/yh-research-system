# 01 Writings - 崔愷

Research date: 2026-05-09

## Main authored / edited works

- `本土设计` / `Local Design` is the most important conceptual label associated with Cui Kai. It frames architecture as rooted in place, climate, culture, material, craft and contemporary life rather than as symbolic decoration. Confidence: high. Sources: CAE profile and multiple interviews.
- `工程报告` / project monographs and design reports associated with Cui Kai and the China Architecture Design & Research Group present his practice through completed public buildings, museums, cultural facilities and urban renewal projects. Confidence: medium-high. Source: book/project listings and CADG/CADRI pages.
- Public essays/interviews repeatedly discuss "本土设计", "绿色建筑", "城市更新", "建筑师的社会责任", "因地制宜", "全生命周期", "运营维护", "反对形式主义". Confidence: high. Sources: official/major media interviews.

## Repeated claims

- A building must grow from its site. It should respond to region, history, climate, use, construction and local life, not paste Chinese symbols onto a generic box.
- Good architecture is not only image; it is an integrated judgment across planning, function, structure, technology, cost, operation and urban responsibility.
- Green building must move from slogans and technical labels to real carbon reduction, passive strategy, operation performance and long-term use.
- Urban renewal should often be "渐进式、修补式、微更新" rather than demolition-and-replacement spectacle.
- Chinese contemporary architecture needs cultural confidence, but that confidence comes from transformation of living tradition, not imitation of ancient forms.

## Candidate mental models

- Place-grown design: site is not background; it is the generator.
- Integrated engineering-aesthetic judgment: architecture is a total coordination problem.
- Anti-symbolic Chineseness: tradition must become spatial logic, material method and life atmosphere.
- Lifecycle realism: design continues into construction, operation, maintenance and aging.
- Urban acupuncture / repair: cities need careful repair more often than heroic replacement.
