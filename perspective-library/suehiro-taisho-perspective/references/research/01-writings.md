## Primary / Near-primary Sources

### Book: `中学受験 ボードゲームで作る学びの土台～経験値は複利～`

- Author reading confirmed as `末廣泰翔（スエヒロタイショウ）`.
- Publisher: エール出版社.
- ISBN: `978-4-7539-3605-2`.
- Public release date: 2026-02-18.
- Public summary frames board games as a foundation for the thinking ability demanded by current middle-school entrance exams.
- The summary contrasts modern entrance exams with rote knowledge and names abilities such as organizing unfamiliar problem statements, expressing one's reasoning in one's own words, and moving forward with one's own grounds.
- Source: https://www.hanmoto.com/bd/isbn/9784753936052

## Repeated Claims

- Board games can produce concrete cognitive experiences, not just entertainment.
- The educational value of a game lies in the kind of thinking its rule system elicits.
- Children should be asked to notice reasons, public information, and consequences.
- Repetition through enjoyable play creates learning without making repetition feel like drill.
- Entrance-exam readiness includes thinking, organizing, explaining, and acting with grounds.
