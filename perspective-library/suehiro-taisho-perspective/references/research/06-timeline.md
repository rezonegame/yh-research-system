# 06 Timeline

## Chronology

### 1985

HMV and related public book-retail snippets identify Suehiro Taisho as born in Kagoshima Prefecture in 1985. Deep Ground profile also situates his origin in Kagoshima.

Sources:

- https://www.hmv.co.jp/artist_%E6%9C%AB%E5%BB%A3%E6%B3%B0%E7%BF%94_000000001012384/media_books_7/
- https://deepground.jp/teachers/

### Schooling

PR TIMES profile states he graduated from Kagoshima Prefectural Tsurumaru High School and Meiji University Law School.

Source: https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000004.000065332.html

### University / Graduate School Period

Deep Ground profile says he moved to Tokyo for university and entered the middle-school entrance exam world around that time. After graduate school he joined Deep Ground.

Source: https://deepground.jp/teachers/

### Deep Ground Reform Period

Public profile describes reforming Deep Ground from a school-specific preparation cram school into a middle-school entrance exam cram school.

Source: https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000004.000065332.html

### 2021

Listed as a speaker for an online home-teaching seminar on 2021-02-20.

Published two signed 樂志館 essays defining thinking, thinking stamina, compounding experience, and board games as teaching material.

Source: https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000004.000065332.html

Additional sources:

- https://deep-ground.jp/2021/06/16/blog/747/
- https://deep-ground.jp/2021/06/23/blog/849/

### 2022-2023

Published multiple BROAD articles in the `ボドゲと子供と教育と` series, connecting specific board games to children's cognitive development and learning.

### 2025

Reported presenting `考える経験値` and `決める経験値` at a teaching-practice research meeting, linking self-decision to outcomes becoming `自分事`.

Source: https://keio-juku-gakudo.hatenablog.jp/entry/kenkyukai

### 2026

Book published: `中学受験 ボードゲームで作る学びの土台～経験値は複利～`, release date 2026-02-18, publisher エール出版社.

Source: https://www.hanmoto.com/bd/isbn/9784753936052

## Phase Interpretation

1. **Early formation:** Kagoshima/Satsuma identity and competitive youth experiences appear in official profile but should not be over-interpreted.
2. **Entrance-exam world entry:** Tokyo university period connects to middle-school entrance education.
3. **Institutional builder:** Deep Ground participation and reform.
4. **Board-game pedagogy articulation:** early Hatena practice reports, 2021 signed conceptual essays, BROAD mechanism studies, a 2025 teaching talk, and the 2026 book turn practice into a public method.

## Evidence Gaps

- Exact dates for university entry, graduate-school completion, and Deep Ground joining were not publicly confirmed in accessible sources.
- Timeline is suitable for broad phase context, not precise biography.
